# Sample Questions for Breadboard AI Tutor

Here are some example questions users might ask:

1. **How do I connect an LED to a breadboard with a resistor?**
   - Learn about basic LED circuits and current-limiting resistors

2. **What is the difference between series and parallel circuits on a breadboard?**
   - Understand circuit configurations and their characteristics

3. **How do I calculate the correct resistor value for my LED?**
   - Learn about Ohm's law and LED specifications

4. **Can you explain how the power rails work on a breadboard?**
   - Understand breadboard anatomy and power distribution

5. **What components do I need to build a simple buzzer circuit?**
   - Get guidance on component selection and circuit design
